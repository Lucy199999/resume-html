Hobbies

I am an extremely active person and love being outdoors. Whilst balancing work and free time, here are some of the hobbies I enjoy:

  • Climbing/Bouldering

    I got into climbing and bouldering in my first year at Nottingham Trent University and love it for the way that it tests my strength in new ways.

  • Roller Skating

    I had always wanted to roller skate, but had always been too afraid to start. Whilst in Nottingham, I pushed myself to go to adult roller skating lessons, which I absolutley loved, for the confidence it gave me, as well as for the friends that I made.

  • Ultimate Frisbee

    I have been playing Ultimate Frisbee for 5 years now, starting when I arrived at De Montfort University. I have loved being part of the frisbee community, which takes great pride in honesty and fair play through its rules of Spirit of the Game.
